No. 03How I Think

Great strategy starts with curiosity.

I don't believe the best strategies begin with answers. They begin with understanding. When you understand the real problem, the right strategy becomes much clearer.

  1. Listen
  2. Understand
  3. Simplify
  4. Align
  5. Execute
  6. Learn
  7. Improve

Step 01Listen

Every project begins with people. I want to hear from employees, customers, sales teams, and leadership before making assumptions. Questions create better strategies than opinions ever will.

Step 02Understand

I look at the entire picture: business goals, revenue, customer behavior, competitive landscape, market trends, and data. Only then do I decide what problem we're actually solving.

Step 03Simplify

Complexity creates confusion, and confusion slows organizations down. My job is to simplify products, messaging, priorities, and processes so teams know exactly what to do next.

Step 04Align

The best strategy fails without alignment. Marketing, sales, creative, product, operations, and leadership should all understand the objective before execution begins.

Step 05Execute

Strategy means nothing without execution. Once the direction is clear, I enjoy building the systems, programs, launches, and campaigns that bring ideas to life.

Step 06Learn

Every project teaches something. I measure results, gather feedback, celebrate wins, and adjust what didn't work.

Step 07Improve

Continuous improvement isn't a phase. It's the job.

No. 06Case Studies

Strategy, proven in the field.

Every engagement follows the same discipline: understand the real problem, build the strategy, align the teams, execute, and measure what happened.

Challenge

Overhaul the compensation and rewards program for 80,000+ independent distributors, where confusion kills momentum and the field's trust is the whole business.

Strategy

Led strategic messaging and launch communications end to end: authored the program manual and directed deployment of 1,000+ assets across 34 markets and 23 languages.

Outcome

Contributed to a 19.5% increase in new Brand Partner enrollments following launch.

Challenge

Launch a flagship product into a loyal but launch-fatigued global customer base, in a category where every claim is regulated.

Strategy

Directed global go-to-market strategy, coordinating 100+ stakeholders across 10+ departments and international regions to deliver launch assets in 34 markets and 23 languages.

Outcome

$770K in first-week sales and the highest sales week in company history.

Challenge

Six product lines, 14+ products, and years of accumulated messaging drift. Every line telling its own story, and none of them telling the brand's.

Strategy

Developed a unified brand voice, messaging architecture, and field-ready copy across the full portfolio, including ASEA VIA, RENUAdvanced, ASEA Performance, REDOXGold, RENU28, and ARS.

Outcome

One voice, one architecture, and compliant field-ready messaging across every product line and customer touchpoint.

Challenge

Hundreds of thousands of customers and associates with no structured journey: no onboarding, no win-back, and revenue dependent on one-off sends.

Strategy

Built the full lifecycle program: a segmented onboarding series with two behavioral journey tracks, plus an automated win-back campaign targeting associates inactive 180+ days.

Outcome

44%+ average email open rates, 60% engagement growth, 80% of campaign revenue driven by automated journeys, and a 3.1% win-back conversion rate, 55% above benchmark.

Challenge

A brand-new SMS channel serving a base of 3.8M+ email subscribers and 100K+ SMS subscribers, with a team of seven running 200+ marketing projects every month.

Strategy

Launched and scaled the SMS program while running lifecycle, retention, and win-back across hundreds of millions of email sends. Redesigned workflows and built a QA program to keep quality high at volume.

Outcome

Grew SMS from $500K to $1.7M+ in annual revenue within one year, sustained a 2.3% average conversion rate, and increased production capacity by 150%.

Challenge

Build a brand from zero with no team, no budget, and 500+ SKUs to source, price, and merchandise.

Strategy

Built and scaled a multi-channel ecommerce business across Amazon, Etsy, and eBay, owning sourcing, merchandising, pricing, inventory, marketplace optimization, and financial planning.

Outcome

A profitable six-figure brand, run for six years. Nothing teaches marketing discipline like spending your own money.

Challenge

How do you evaluate a growing DTC wellness brand's lifecycle opportunity from the outside in?

Strategy

Built an independent strategic audit: competitive analysis against the category leader, a full lifecycle opportunity map, and a 30/60/90 day activation plan.

Outcome

A complete strategy workbook demonstrating how I diagnose, prioritize, and plan before day one. Available to walk through live.
